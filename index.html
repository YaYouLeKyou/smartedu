SMART TRANSFORMATION IN EDUCATION WITH ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

Empowering Education with AI – Erasmus+ Small-Scale Partnership

About the Project

Project Acronym: Smart edu AI

This Erasmus+ project, "Smart Transformation in Education with Artificial Intelligence" (Smart edu AI), is a small-scale partnership in school education. It will run for 24 months, from January 1, 2025, to December 31, 2026, with a total lump sum of €60,000.00.

The project's motivation is to enhance student development and learning experiences by integrating AI technologies into educational processes. It addresses the necessity of AI in improving educational quality, ensuring equal opportunities, and meeting individual learning needs in the digital age.

Participating Organizations

Eurolab for Teachers (Poland)

Coordinator with expertise in modern pedagogy, gamification, and sustainability-focused AI initiatives.

Düzce Milli Egemenlik (Türkiye)

Public institution for gifted students, specializing in research, teacher training, and AI project testing.

France Culture Connection (France)

NGO supporting disadvantaged groups through educational, cultural, and digital projects, with AI and ethics expertise.

Centro Público Integrado O Cruce (Spain)

Multilingual public center recognized for innovative teaching, focusing on disadvantaged students and AI impact analysis.

Core Objectives

Using Student-Centered AI Applications

Personalized learning experiences tailored to individual learning styles and paces. Adaptive AI tools enhance learning management and improve student engagement.

AI Training for Teachers

Improving educators' digital skills for more effective and personalized teaching. Addresses the global gap in AI tool readiness among teachers.

Learning Analysis & Feedback

AI-based data analysis to identify student strengths and weaknesses, allowing tailored teaching strategies for better outcomes.

Ensuring Equality of Opportunity

Breaking down barriers for disadvantaged students through AI solutions, improving performance regardless of background or abilities.

Key Activities Planned

AI Training for Educators (France)

7-day program covering AI integration in teaching, lesson plans, AI-based tools, performance tracking, ethical AI, and digital pedagogy.

Artificial Intelligences Combine (Spain)

Focus on personalized learning for disadvantaged and migrant students aged 12-15 using AI for language and subject learning support.

Art of Science with AI (Türkiye)

AI-enhanced science experiments, music composition, and visual design activities fostering creativity, critical thinking, and tech literacy.

AI & Environmental Protection (Poland)

Using AI to raise environmental awareness, calculate carbon footprints, and design eco-friendly cities in interactive simulations.
